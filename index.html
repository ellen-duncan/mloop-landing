Common questions

Is this really free?
Yes. mLOOP is free for loan officers. We're paid by lenders when a match leads to a conversation you choose to have.
Will my current employer find out?
Not from us. Your identity stays private until you opt in to share it with a specific lender. No public profile, no notifications, no leaks.
Why do you need my NMLS number?
To confirm you're a licensed loan officer. It's never shown to lenders and never used for marketing.
How is this different from a recruiter?
A recruiter pushes you toward whoever is paying them. mLOOP shows you matches based on what you said matters — and lets you stay anonymous until you don't want to be.
How long does it take?
About 60 seconds to sign up. You'll see initial matches the same day.
